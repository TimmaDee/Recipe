A Jolly Good Burger

This is a burger of my own creation, this is the first time the method has ever been shared

Ingredients

  • 250g of lean minced beef
  • 100g of spinach
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 slices of American cheese
  • Garlic and herb mayonnaise
  • 2 brioche buns
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • The secret ingredient; OXO beef stock cubes

That's right, the secret ingredient is OXO Beef stock cubes.
They give the meat an extra kick of flavour that's hard to beat.

Method

  1. Heat a dry pan on the highest heat

  2. Form 2 balls from the mince, then flatten them out gently into patties
    It is important to not overwork the mince

  3. Season the patties with salt, pepper and the OXO cube

  4. Use a basting brush, or your hands if you're feeling whimsical,
    and coat both sides of the patties in extra virgin olive oil

  5. Is that pan at optimum temperature?
    If it is, time to drop the patties onto it
    You should get a nice sear almost instantaneously,
    we can't check though as the burger needs to brown

  6. Toast the brioche buns on level 2

  7. Once the buns are down plop some garlic and herb mayonnaise onto the bottom bun

  8. Put the spinach directly onto the mayonnaise
    You may be wondering why we have done it in this order
    It is so the mayonnaise can keep the spinach from exiting the burger

  9. After 5 minutes the, flip the burgers over
    They should be nicely browned

  10. After two minutes of cooking on the other side place the cheese on top

  11. Now it's just a case of waiting for the cheese to melt into the burger
    like a... melted cheese onto a burger
  12. Once the cheese is melted, check the bottom of the patty is browned
    and place it on the bottom bun

  13. Place the top bun on the burger

  14. Eat the burger

Mission accomplished
